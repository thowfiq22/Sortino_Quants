While helpful, it is not mandatory. Our curriculum is designed to take you from foundational concepts to advanced quantitative modeling. However, a disciplined mindset and a willingness to engage with data are requirements.
Is there a performance guarantee? expand_more
In the institutional world, "guarantees" are a red flag. We provide you with the same frameworks used by top firms, but execution and performance are dependent on individual discipline and market conditions. Trading involves significant risk.
