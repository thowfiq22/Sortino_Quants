# Design System & Specification: Sortino Quants

This document defines the branding, styles, and page layouts for **Sortino Quants**, a premium trading education and community platform based in the UK.

---

## Brand Positioning & Persona

*   **Brand Name**: Sortino Quants
*   **Branding Style**: Elite quantitative trading firm, institutional broker, or premium financial academy (resembling a Bloomberg Terminal interface or high-end fintech brand).
*   **Tone**: Disciplined, professional, data-driven, risk-aware, educational, and premium.
*   **Anti-Scam Guideline**: Strictly **avoid** a signal-selling, get-rich-quick, or gambling/casino appearance. Focus on structured learning and quantitative thinking.
*   **Target Audience**: Beginner to intermediate day traders, gold, forex, crypto, and stock traders seeking professional mentorship.

---

## Visual Design System

### 1. Colors
*   **Primary Background**: `#050607` (Deepest Charcoal Black)
*   **Secondary Background**: `#0B0D10` (Off-black Slate)
*   **Card Background**: `#11151B` (Rich Dark Blue-Grey)
*   **Primary Gold Accent**: `#D6A84F` (Soft Metallic Gold)
*   **Highlight Gold Accent**: `#F6D27A` (Bright Metallic Gold)
*   **Deep Gold Accent**: `#9C6B21` (Warm Bronze/Brown Gold)
*   **Primary Text**: `#F8F5EC` (Warm Off-white)
*   **Secondary Text**: `#B8B8B2` (Muted Warm Grey)
*   **Border Stroke**: `#2A2E35` (Sleek Dark Border)
*   **Success Indicator**: `#22C55E` (Emerald Green)
*   **Warning/Risk Indicator**: `#EF4444` (Crimson Red)
*   **Chart/Data Accent**: `#38BDF8` (Bloomberg Cyan Blue)

### 2. Typography
*   **Headings**: `Sora` (Geometric, premium, bold sans-serif)
*   **Body Copy**: `Inter` (Highly legible UI font)
*   **Numbers & Data**: `JetBrains Mono` or `Share Tech Mono` (Institutional quantitative look)

### 3. Logo & Visual Identity
*   **Logo Crop Guidelines**: Crop the brand logo to display only the **Arrow Symbol** and the **SORTINO QUANTS** text. Remove any decorative frames, lines, or icons at the bottom of the poster.
*   **Tagline**: Display the tagline as text below the logo:
    `Position Yourself on the Efficient Frontier`
*   **Visual Elements**: Use the "Efficient Frontier" curve from the logo (a risk-return trajectory line) as a recurrent design language throughout the pages, using subtle gold curves and quantitative plotting points instead of generic stock charts.
*   **Founder Photo Consistency**: Maintain matching portrait treatments and professional lighting across all founder photos. Do not mix illustrated styles with photographic ones.

## Global Navigation & Layout

*   **Header (Navigation)**:
    *   Logo (Cropped arrow symbol + SORTINO QUANTS)
    *   Links: Home, Meet the Team, Memberships, Contact
    *   CTA Button: "Explore Memberships" (Solid Gold, Sora font, bold)
*   **Footer**:
    *   Links to Risk Disclaimer, Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions.
    *   Copyright and static company details.
    *   A persistent, highly visible educational warning message:
        *"Derivative trading involves high risk. Content is strictly for educational purposes."*
