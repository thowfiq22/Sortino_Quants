About Sathish

Sathish is a UK-based business and technology professional with an MBA in Finance and over 15 years of experience across business analysis, product management, IT consulting, digital transformation, and enterprise solution delivery.

His professional background spans banking, insurance, financial services, enterprise technology, and digital product ecosystems, where he has contributed to large-scale transformation initiatives, data-driven platforms, operational optimisation, and strategic business solutions.

In addition to his corporate experience, Sathish is also an entrepreneur and technology enthusiast with a strong passion for financial markets, AI-driven analytics, and digital innovation.
