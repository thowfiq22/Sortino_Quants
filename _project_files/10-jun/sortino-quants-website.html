Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Do you provide financial advice or manage money?
No. Sortino Quants Ltd is a financial markets education and research platform. We do not provide investment advice, portfolio management, or any regulated financial advisory services. All content — including analyst perspectives, trade setups, and market commentary — is provided solely for educational and informational purposes.
What is the difference between membership and 1-to-1 sessions?
Membership (£48/month) gives you access to our full educational community: live sessions, market research, analyst content, and ongoing learning resources. The 1-to-1 sessions are private, structured mentorships tailored specifically to your level — Beginner (4 sessions/£600), Intermediate (5 sessions/£750), or Advanced (3 sessions/£450). Members receive 50% off 1-to-1 session rates.
Do I need a maths or finance background to join?
Not at all. Our Beginner pathway starts from the foundations — market structure, risk thinking, platform setup — and builds systematically. A disciplined mindset and a genuine commitment to learning are the only requirements.
Are your affiliate and broker relationships disclosed?
Yes — always. We maintain full transparency over all commercial relationships, including CPA affiliate commissions and Introducing Broker (IB) arrangements. These are documented in our Revenue Model & Transparency page. Our educational recommendations are never driven by these commercial arrangements.
What does a Discovery Call involve?
A Discovery Call is a short, free conversation with one of our mentors before you book a 1-to-1 pathway. It helps us understand your current trading background, learning goals, and which pathway is the right fit. There is no obligation to purchase and no sales pressure.
Can I cancel my membership at any time?
Yes. SQ Membership is a monthly rolling subscription with no long-term commitment. You can cancel at any time without penalty.
